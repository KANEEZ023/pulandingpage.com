Bachelor Courses

Our university offers a wide range of bachelor's degree programs to cater to various academic interests and career goals.The courses include Bachelor of Science (BSc), Bachelor of Education (BEd), Bachelor of Pharmacy (B.Pharm), Bachelor of Commerce (BCom), Bachelor of Arts (BA), Integrated Chemistry,and more.

Professional Courses

Our university offers a range of professional courses tailored to student's career aspirations. Programs like MCA specialize in advanced computer programming, while the MBA focuses on business leadership and management. The M.Ed prepares individuals for educational leadership roles, and the MSW trains social issues and provide support.

Post Graduate Courses

Our university offers a range of post-graduate programs for specialized education. The MSc program provides scientific training in biology, chemistry, and physics, while the MPharm focuses on advanced research. Frequently Asked Questions!

What is the NAAC grade of Palamuru University?

The university is accredited by NAAC with a B grade and was approved by Union Grant Commission UGC.

What is the history of Palamuru University?

Palamuru University has been established in the year 2008 as a State University by statutory enactment to cater to the growing requirements of Higher Education of the region in general and of Mahabubnagar district in particular.

About Us

Palamuru University has been established in the year 2008 as a State University by statutory enactment to cater to the growing
requirements of Higher Education in the region in general and of the Mahabubnagar district in particular.The University
started functioning from the academic year 2008-09 with the then PG College of Osmania University at Mahabubnagar as the nucleus,
where in five PG courses were being offered.
