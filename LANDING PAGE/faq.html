What is the NAAC grade of Palamuru University?

The university is accredited by NAAC with a B grade and was approved by Union Grant Commission UGC.

What is the history of Palamuru University?

Palamuru University has been established in the year 2008 as a State University by statutory enactment to cater to the growing requirements of Higher Education of the region in general and of Mahabubnagar district in particular.
