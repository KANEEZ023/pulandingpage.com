About Us

The Top State University in Mahabubnagar in Telangana.

Palamuru University, established in 2008 as a State University, caters to the educational needs of Mahabubnagar district. Initially operating from Osmania University's PG College, it offered five PG courses. The university introduced new regular and self-financing courses, along with faculty recruitment. Infrastructure development was prioritized, leading to the construction of administrative, academic, library, and pharmacy buildings, as well as separate hostels for boys and girls. The university also focused on creating academic facilities, student amenities, and a grand entrance arch in subsequent years.

About Us

Palamuru University has been established in the year 2008 as a State University by statutory enactment to cater to the growing
requirements of Higher Education in the region in general and of the Mahabubnagar district in particular.The University
started functioning from the academic year 2008-09 with the then PG College of Osmania University at Mahabubnagar as the nucleus,
where in five PG courses were being offered.
